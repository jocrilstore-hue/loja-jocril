# Quick Checks

Fast diagnostic checklists for rapid assessment without a full audit. Run these when you need a quick pass or want to triage before deciding if a full review is warranted.

---

## Hierarchy Tests

- **Squint Test**: Blur your vision. Can you still see the visual hierarchy?
- **Grayscale Test**: Does hierarchy hold without color?
- **Weight Test**: Is hierarchy created with size alone, or also weight/color/spacing?

---

## Krug's Tests (Don't Make Me Think)

- **Trunk Test**: Dropped on a random page — do you know: what site? what page? main sections? how to search? how to get home?
- **Happy Talk Test**: Is there fluff text that says nothing? Kill it.
- **Question Mark Test**: Does anything make users pause to think? (bad)
- **Obvious Test**: Is it self-evident what to click and what happens next?

---

## Psychology Tests (Weinschenk)

- **4-Item Test**: Are navigation/choices limited to ~4 items?
- **Face Direction Test**: Do faces look toward CTAs, not away?
- **Social Proof Test**: Is there evidence others have done this?
- **Progress Test**: Do users know where they are in a process?
- **Choice Overload Test**: Too many options causing paralysis?

---

## Layout Tests

- **Thumb Zone Test**: Are primary CTAs reachable on mobile?
- **Reading Order**: Tab through. Does focus order make sense?
- **Spacing Test**: Is there more space between groups than within?

---

## Typography & Alignment Tests

- **Alignment Consistency**: Same content type = same alignment? (Check each section)
- **Centered Body Check**: Is any body text (3+ lines) centered? (bad — hard to read)
- **Left Edge Test**: Scroll down — does text maintain consistent left alignment?
- **Type Scale Count**: More than 7–8 font sizes? Needs consolidation.
- **Line Length Check**: Any paragraphs wider than 75 characters?

---

## Color Tests

- **60-30-10 Check**: Is there a clear dominant (60%), secondary (30%), accent (10%) distribution?
- **Greyscale Test**: Convert to greyscale — does hierarchy hold without hue?
- **Section Switch Test**: Does each background color change correspond to a content change?
- **Grey-on-Color Check**: Is pure grey text used on colored backgrounds? (bad — needs undertone)
- **Palette Count**: More than 15 unique colors (including shades)? Needs consolidation.
- **Contrast Spot Check**: Sample body text — does it hit 4.5:1?
- **Colorblind Test**: Is color the ONLY indicator of meaning anywhere? (bad)

---

## Refactoring UI Quick Wins

- **Border Audit**: Can any borders be replaced with shadows or spacing?
- **Shadow Check**: Are shadows offset vertically (not spread evenly)?
- **Grey-on-Color**: Is grey text used on colored backgrounds? (bad)
- **Icon Size**: Are any icons blown up beyond their intended size?
- **System Check**: Do spacing/font values come from a constrained scale?
