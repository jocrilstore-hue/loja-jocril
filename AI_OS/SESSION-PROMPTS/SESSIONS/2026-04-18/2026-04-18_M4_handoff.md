---

## Architecture note

`not-found.tsx` and `error.tsx` live at `app/` level (not inside `(store)/`), so they don't inherit `StoreThemeProvider` from the store layout. They each instantiate their own provider. This is intentional — these are catch-all pages that may trigger from any route, including admin routes.
