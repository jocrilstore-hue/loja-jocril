## What must NOT regress

- `StoreHeader` uses `ThemeToggle` imported from `./StoreTheme` — do not reintroduce `window.ThemeToggle` (the prototype pattern).
- All admin pages MUST mount inside `<AdminShell active={...} breadcrumbs={...}>`; the `active` prop uses `AdminActiveKey` union — extend the union if you add new sidebar items.
- `AdminShell` forces dark theme on mount. The storefront uses `StoreThemeProvider` (context-based) instead.
- `--color-destructive` is defined in `app/globals.css`, not in the prototype's token file. Keep it there.
- Next 16 auto-modified `tsconfig.json` (jsx → react-jsx) and `next-env.d.ts` on first build — accepted, do not revert.
